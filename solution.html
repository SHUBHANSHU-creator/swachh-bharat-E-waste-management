SOLUTIONS

SOME ECO-FRIENDLY WASTE DISPOSAL TECHNIQUES BY OUR GROUP THAT YOU CAN USE TO DISPOSE E-WASTE LOCALLY

1. Give Your Electronic Waste to a Certified E-Waste Recycler.​

You need to find an e-waste recycler who is officially certified by the Basel Action Network (BAN). BAN is a non-profit organization of recycling companies which are dedicated to recycling e-waste in a safe and responsible way.​

​2. Sell Off Your Outdated Technology

​One man’s junk is another man’s treasure as the old saying goes. This can be applied to helping you get rid of your old electronics

​3.Donating Your Outdated Technology

​Old gadgets that you no longer need can be donated as they may be useful to others. Your old computer may be useful to either an NGO or students

4.Substitution

​certain substitutions should be made for the harmful materials which are released in the environment from e-waste like cadmium, mercury

5.Dumping waste can be hazardous

​Dumping of e-waste should be stopped as it releases harmful chemicals into environment and personal information can also be compromised.
